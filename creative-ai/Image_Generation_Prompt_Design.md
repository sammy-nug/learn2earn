# 🎯 Objective: Create Prompts for Models That Generate Images

---

## 1️⃣ Write a descriptive prompt for an image model (e.g., "A futuristic city skyline at sunset in watercolor style.").

> A futuristic city skyline at sunset in watercolor style.

---

## 2️⃣ Refine the prompt by adding constraints such as art style, colors, or perspective.

> A futuristic city skyline at sunset in watercolor style, viewed from a high rooftop perspective. Use warm orange, pink, and purple tones in the sky, with soft reflections on glass skyscrapers. Include flying vehicles and subtle atmospheric haze for depth.

---

#  Comparison of Results

| Aspect | Vague Prompt | Refined Prompt |
|---------|--------------|---------------|
| Visual Consistency | Low | High |
| Style Definition | Undefined | Clearly watercolor |
| Color Control | Random | Warm sunset palette |
| Perspective | Unspecified | High rooftop angle |
| Scene Depth | Basic | Enhanced with haze & reflections |
| Creative Direction | Broad | Focused and intentional |
