# **Objective:** Guide the model to solve a problem with explicit reasoning.
---

## 1. Write a logic puzzle (e.g., "Alice is older than Bob, Bob is older than Carol. Who is the youngest?").
Alice is older than Bob, Bob is older than Carol. Who is the youngest?

## 2. Prompt the model: "Explain your reasoning before giving the final answer."
**Prompt:**
Read the following logic puzzle carefully. Explain your reasoning step by step before giving the final answer.

## 3. Verify that the model provides step-by-step reasoning followed by the correct answer.
**Step-by-step reasoning:**

- Alice is older than Bob → Alice > Bob

- Bob is older than Carol → Bob > Carol

- Therefore: Alice > Bob > Carol

- The youngest person is the one at the bottom of this order

### Final Answer:
Carol is the youngest.
